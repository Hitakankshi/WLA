Top Historical & Spiritual Sites

Buddhist Pilgrimage

Bodh Gaya

The epicenter of Buddhism and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, this is where Siddhartha Gautama attained enlightenment under the Bodhi Tree. Home to the magnificent Mahabodhi Temple.

Ancient University

Nalanda Mahavihara

Explore the ruins of one of the world's oldest residential universities. Nalanda was a global center for learning and knowledge, flourishing between the 5th and 12th centuries.

Learn More →
Historical & Natural

Rajgir

The first capital of the Magadha Kingdom, surrounded by picturesque hills. Visit the Vishwa Shanti Stupa (Peace Pagoda) and enjoy the scenic new Glass Bridge.

Learn More →
Sikh Pilgrimage

Takht Sri Harmandir Sahib Ji

Located in Patna, this is one of the five primary Takhts of Sikhism, marking the revered birthplace of the tenth Sikh Guru, Guru Gobind Singh.

Learn More →
Jain Pilgrimage

Jal Mandir, Pawapuri

A stunning marble temple situated in the middle of a large lotus pond. It is the sacred site where Lord Mahavira, the 24th Tirthankara, attained Nirvana.

Learn More →
Architectural Marvel

Sher Shah Suri Tomb

Located in Sasaram, this grand Indo-Islamic mausoleum is built in the middle of an artificial lake, showcasing brilliant 16th-century architecture.
